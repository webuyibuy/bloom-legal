Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 5, 2026
Effective Date: February 5, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Bloom ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

When you use Bloom, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Email address and profile information when you sign up using Google Sign-In
  • User-Generated Content: Tasks, routines, challenges, and notes you create within the App
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, including task completions and app features used

2. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Log Data: Error logs and performance metrics (if you opt-in to error reporting)
  • Authentication Tokens: Securely stored tokens for maintaining your session

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Service Delivery: Providing and maintaining the App's functionality
  • Personalization: Customizing your experience and remembering your preferences
  • Communication: Sending notifications about your tasks and challenges (if enabled)
  • Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to improve the App
  • Security: Protecting against unauthorized access and fraudulent activity
  • AI Features: Processing your requests for AI-powered task generation (if you use this feature)

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms:

  • iOS: Keychain for sensitive data
  • Android: Keystore for sensitive data
  • AsyncStorage for non-sensitive app preferences

Cloud Storage (Supabase)

When you're online, we sync your data to our secure cloud database (Supabase) to:

  • Enable access across multiple devices
  • Provide backup and recovery
  • Support real-time synchronization
Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security practices including:
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) policies ensuring you can only access your own data
  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication via Google OAuth
  • Regular security audits and updates

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Google Sign-In

Used for authentication. Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Supabase

Our backend database provider. Supabase's privacy policy applies: https://supabase.com/privacy

OpenAI (Optional)

If you use AI task generation features, your prompts are processed by OpenAI. OpenAI's privacy policy applies: https://openai.com/privacy

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share information
  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who help us operate the App (Supabase, Google, OpenAI)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

Access and Portability

  • View all your data within the App
  • Request a copy of your data

Correction and Deletion

  • Edit or delete your tasks, routines, and challenges directly in the App
  • Request complete account deletion by contacting us

Opt-Out Options

  • Notifications: Disable push notifications in device settings
  • AI Features: Simply don't use AI-powered features
  • Analytics: Contact us to opt out of usage analytics

Data Retention

  • Active Data: Retained while you use the App
  • Deleted Data: Soft-deleted data is permanently removed after 30 days
  • Account Deletion: All data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion request

Children's Privacy

Bloom is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending a notification if changes are significant

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@bloomapp.com
  • Support: https://webuyibuy.github.io/bloom-legal/
