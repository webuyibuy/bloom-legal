Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 5, 2026
Last Updated: February 5, 2026

Introduction

Bloom ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Bloom mobile application.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

  • Email address (when you sign in with Google)
  • Google ID (for authentication purposes)
  • Display name and profile image (from your Google account)

2. User-Generated Content

  • Tasks and subtasks you create
  • Routines you set up
  • Challenge history and completion data
  • AI conversation transcripts (for task generation features)

3. Usage Data

  • AI feature usage (session duration, number of tasks generated)
  • App settings and preferences
  • Notification preferences

4. Technical Data

  • Device information (OS version, app version)
  • Crash reports and error logs (to improve app stability)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  1. Provide core functionality: Store and sync your tasks, routines, and challenges
  2. Enable AI features: Process voice input to generate tasks and subtasks
  3. Send notifications: Deliver reminders for routines and challenge updates (with your permission)
  4. Improve the app: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  5. Ensure security: Authenticate users and protect against unauthorized access

Data Storage and Security

Storage Location

  • Backend: Supabase (hosted on secure cloud infrastructure)
  • Local storage: Encrypted on your device using iOS Keychain or Android Keystore

Security Measures

  • All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL
  • Authentication tokens are stored in secure encrypted storage
  • Row Level Security (RLS) policies ensure users can only access their own data
  • Passwords are never stored; we use secure Google OAuth authentication

Third-Party Services

Bloom integrates with the following third-party services:

1. Supabase (Backend & Database)

  • Purpose: Data storage, authentication, real-time sync
  • Data shared: All user-generated content and account information
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

2. Google Sign-In

  • Purpose: User authentication
  • Data shared: Email, Google ID, display name, profile image
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. OpenAI (AI Features)

  • Purpose: Voice transcription and task generation
  • Data shared: Voice recordings (transcribed), conversation context
  • Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy
  • Note: Voice recordings are NOT stored; only transcriptions are processed

Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted tasks/routines: Soft-deleted items are permanently removed after 90 days
  • Account deletion: You can request full account deletion by contacting us

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  1. Access your data: View all data associated with your account
  2. Update your data: Edit tasks, routines, and profile information
  3. Delete your data: Request complete account and data deletion
  4. Export your data: Request a copy of your data in portable format
  5. Opt-out of notifications: Disable notifications in app settings

Children's Privacy

Bloom is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending a notification if changes are significant

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@bloom.withema.com
  • Website: Bloom.withema.com
Consent: By using Bloom, you consent to this Privacy Policy and our collection and use of information as described herein.
